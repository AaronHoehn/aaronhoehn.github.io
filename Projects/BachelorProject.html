Can AI NPCs influence users decisions?

For my bachelor’s project, I designed and conducted an experimental study comparing AI-driven NPCs with traditionally scripted NPCs. The goal of the research was to determine whether AI-controlled characters have a stronger impact on users and whether they are capable of influencing player decisions more effectively than pre-scripted characters.

The experiment was set in a VR courtroom scenario created with Unity. In this setting, the player assumed the role of a judge and interacted with both traditional and AI-driven NPCs. After hearing their statements and observing their behavior, the player was required to decide on the severity of the sentence for each character.
