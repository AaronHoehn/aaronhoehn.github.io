GoethesPuppetTheater

- 5th Semester Group Project

Goethes Puppet Theater is an interactive Mixed Reality experience developed for the Goethe Museum by a team of three. Users place physical puppet, onto a miniature stage to trigger AI-driven, dynamic plays featuring characters from Johann Wolfgang von Goethe’s life and works.

As a generalist on the team, I was involved in all core areas of development. We combined room mapping and NavMesh-based navigation with ChatGPT-generated dialogues that dynamically adapt to structured prompts (controlling theme, emotion, and backdrop).

Key Features: AI-driven storytelling, Meta Voice SDK (TTS), 80+ custom mocap animations, cloth physics and ragdoll physics.
