Saving Angkor War (2024)

Unity Mixed Reality Multiplayer Photon PUN2

3rd Semester Group Project

For our 3rd-semester project, we selected Angkor Wat as our cultural heritage site, exploring the theme of "Over-reality". In our game/experience, players are tasked with saving the temples by preserving them through the strategic redirection of water canals.

We were five people in our group, and my task was to handle the VR interactions such as chopping and crafting bamboo, placing dams, lifting stone, and balloon control. Additionally, I also took care of level design, bug fixing, and asset modeling.
