Internship at BluSum Solutions

In 2024, I completed a four-month internship at BluSum Solutions in Heidelberg, Germany. During this time, I worked on the development of a virtual reality (VR) application designed to support the therapy of patients with ADHD.

The project focused on creating immersive and interactive environments that help patients improve attention, focus, and cognitive control through guided therapeutic exercises. As part of the development team, I contributed to the design and implementation of multiple VR scenes, each tailored to specific therapeutic goals.
