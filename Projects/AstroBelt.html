Astro Belt (2022)

Unity Retro Game

Personal Solo Project

This is the first game I have created using the Unity Engine. I got introduced to C# for the first time and gained further knowledge about the basics of object-oriented programming, as well as VFX, post processing and low-poly 3D modeling. The concept is very simple and reminiscent of the classic games played in the last century. The objective is to beat your high score by dodging asteroids and destroying them to accumulate points.
