BattleWall (2025)

Unity Mixed Reality Multiplayer Photon PUN2

6th Semester Group Project

Battlewall is an arcade-style mixed reality multiplayer game designed for both players and spectators on a shared big screen. Two players compete by throwing virtual units onto their side of a physical powerwall, spawning them into an ongoing battle where strategic unit placement is key.

Power-ups provide bonuses during gameplay, while a third participant from the audience can influence the match by triggering interactive world events using a controller, creating a dynamic and engaging experience for everyone involved.

This project was developed in Unity by a team of three.

Tools & Technologies

Unity
Photon PUN2
Blender
